Phishing Scams
What is phishing?

Phishing is a type of online scam where criminals impersonate legitimate organizations via email, text message, advertisement or other means in order to steal sensitive information.

How to spot phishing attempts:
  • Urgent or threatening language demanding immediate action
  • Requests for personal information like passwords or SSN
  • Poor spelling and grammar
  • Generic greetings like "Dear Customer" instead of your name
  • Suspicious links that don't match the organization's real website
Example Phishing Email

From: support@yourbank.com

Subject: Urgent: Your account has been locked

Dear Customer,

We've detected unusual activity on your account. To prevent your account from being permanently locked, please verify your information by clicking the link below:

http://yourbank-secure.com/login

If you don't verify within 24 hours, your account will be suspended.

Sincerely,
Your Bank Security Team

This is a fake email designed to steal your login credentials.

Protection tips:
  • Never click links in unsolicited emails
  • Verify website URLs before entering information
  • Use two-factor authentication
  • Keep software and browsers updated
  • Look for HTTPS and padlock icon in browser
  • When in doubt, contact the company directly
